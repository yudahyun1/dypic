More Pixels. More Detail. Inspired by ISOCELL

Why smartphone cameras are constantly getting better

Samsung Electronics developed 108MP and 200MP ISOCELL image sensors to help people take brilliantly detailed, ultra-high resolution photos using only their smartphones. We wondered: what’s the best way to show people how powerful ISOCELL sensors can be? We decided to create a specially designed photobooth so people could see for themselves how detailed photos taken with these sensors are.

Image sensors
at the heart of
your smartphone
camera

Ever wonder why image sensors are so
important to the performance of smartphone
cameras? It’s all about size and quality
including detail. With up to 200 million pixels,
the ISOCELL image sensor lets users take
absolutely massive photos of up to approx.
16,000 by 12,000 pixels. Since a photo taken in
these dimensions is 16 times bigger than those
taken in 12MP, it will have far more details than
images taken with other smartphone cameras.

Photobooth
powered by
ISOCELL image sensors

At the end of the day, it’s the sensor, more than any other component, t hat determines the performance of a smartphone camera. To show exactly what this means, Samsung installed a pixel-dense smartphone camera sensor in an old photobooth. We thought that people would be surprised by how detailed ultra-high resolution sensors can be – not what you’d expectfrom an old photobooth.

How Big and Detailed
Smartphone Images
Can Be

Rather than a photo, the photobooth gave people who took pictures a
printout that said, “look up.” When they left the photobooth, they saw
the ultra-high resolution photo of themselves printed on a 45 meter
by 18 meter billboard nearby. Notably, when photos are zoomed-in on
a billboard, it showed users the quality of 200MP photos at 100% of
the original size. Check out the lifelike details of eyes, hairs and skin.

With this fun and illuminating demonstration,
participants discovered the real power of
ultra-high

means more
highly-detailed photos.
As sensors with both 200MP and 108MP ultra
high-resolution become more
common,
the quality of smartphone sphotography will
